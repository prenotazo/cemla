2) yes it's possible, that's what's gon is about, in 1 school a subscription not marked as gon and in another school marked as gon. That's why 1 school will sent and the other not otherwise it's sent twice.

FYI - what are gon-students?
A gon-student is a student who has some troubles at school like e.g. when a student is autistic. But it's not because a student is autistic that he/she is stupid. Therefore this student goes to a "normal" school = subscribed in MS 111 or 211 and not marked as GON. Because the student is autistic he/she can go to a normal school but he/she still needs some extra help, some extra support. That support will be given by a school of special education, therefore this student also will be subscribed in a school with MS 121/221 but is marked as GON. This means the student goes to a normal school, but just for support he/she can count on the school of special education.
